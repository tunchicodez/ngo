About Us

Honoring a legacy of compassion, service, and transformational impact across Ghana.

Our Founding Story

The Annor Yeboah Care Foundation was established to continue the inspired legacy of the Late Bishop Dr. Augustine Annor Yeboah, a visionary leader whose life was dedicated to uplifting others through education, spirituality, and community service.

Bishop Dr. Annor-Yeboah believed that investing in people—particularly through education and skills development—was the most powerful way to transform communities. His philanthropic work touched countless lives across Ghana, providing opportunities for those who needed them most.

Today, his family and dedicated trustees carry forward this mission, ensuring that his vision of hope and transformation continues to flourish. Through our three core programs, we honor his memory by creating pathways to success for students, apprentices, and families in need.

Our Vision

To continue the inspired legacy of Late Bishop Dr. Augustine Annor Yeboah by creating a Ghana where every individual has access to quality education, vocational training, and the support needed to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To improve lives by investing in people through education, vocational training, and donations. We are committed to providing sustainable support that empowers individuals and strengthens communities across Ghana.

Legal Credentials & Recognition

Limited by Guarantee

Officially registered as a Limited by Guarantee organization in 2015, ensuring proper governance and accountability.

NPO License

Licensed by Ghana's Non-Profit Organisation Secretariat for the 2025-2026 period, demonstrating our commitment to transparency and compliance.

Commitment to Transparency

As a registered non-profit organization, we maintain the highest standards of financial accountability and program transparency. Our operations are governed by a dedicated board of trustees and directors who ensure that every contribution is used effectively to achieve our mission.
